About Us

Barcelona Football Academy, established in 2017, is a premium football training facility located at the Racecourse Stadium in Colombo 7, Sri Lanka. The academy is the brainchild of Kamaldeen Kabeer, a renowned national football player who has a passion for developing young talents in the sport. The academy provides an environment that is conducive to the growth of young football players and helps them to reach their full potential. The facilities at the academy are of the highest standards and include well-manicured playing fields, state-of-the-art training equipment, and modern changing rooms. The academy is equipped to provide training to players of all ages and abilities, and its experienced coaching staff is dedicated to delivering quality coaching to help players improve their skills. The academy's training program is designed to develop players both physically and mentally. The program is structured to cover various aspects of the game, including technical skills, tactical awareness, fitness, and nutrition. The coaches at the academy use innovative techniques and exercises to help players improve their skills and reach their full potential. The academy also provides a strong emphasis on the importance of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship, and the coaches work closely with players to help them develop these qualities. In addition to the training program, the academy also organizes various tournaments and competitions for players to participate in.
These events provide an opportunity for players to showcase their skills and compete against players from other academies. The academy also arranges friendly matches with teams from other countries, giving players the chance to experience international competition. The academy has a strong focus on player development and has a proven track record of producing talented players who go on to represent their country at the international level. Many of the academy's graduates have received scholarships to top football schools in Europe, and several have gone on to play professionally in Europe and Asia. In conclusion, Barcelona Football Academy is a leading football training facility that provides a comprehensive program for players of all ages and abilities. With its state-of-the-art facilities, experienced coaching staff, and focus on player development, the academy is committed to helping players reach their full potential and achieve their goals in football. If you are passionate about football and want to take your skills to the next level, then Barcelona Football Academy is the place for you.
